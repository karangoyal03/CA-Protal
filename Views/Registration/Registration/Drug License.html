Introduction



Drug license by the competent authority under Drugs and Cosmetic Act, 1940 is mandatory to carry out a business concerning drugs/medicines or cosmetics.Drug Control Department is a regulatory authority which regulates the manufacture of medicines & cosmetics, storage, distribution and sale of medicines.With the purpose of including Ayurvedic& Unani drugs, The act was later amended in the year 1964. There are three kinds of license, the retail license, wholesale license for drug distribution or sale in India and Restricted license for Gen.Store.
